Phelgm

Phlegm is the thick, sticky mucus that your body makes, especially when you're sick. It comes from your lungs and throat and is usually clear but can turn yellow, green, or even brown if you have an infection. Your body makes phlegm to trap dust, germs, and other unwanted stuff, helping to keep your lungs clean.
